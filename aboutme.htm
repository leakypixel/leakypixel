About me

I'm a front-end developer by profession, but I like to get my claws into anything that excites me - whether that be chemistry, programming or generally exploring in any form.

My bread and butter is CSS and HTML, I also like to play with JavaScript and JQuery - as well as anything new that catches my eye. I especially like the challenge of responsive design and learning new web technologies.

I'm also into amateur rocketry, something I carried over from my love of chemistry. I've always enjoyed practical chemistry, and persued it academically alongside computing for a time, before my love of building web projects took over and I went to full time computing.

I spend a lot of my time outdoors, camping, climbing and hiking, as well as a bit of urban exploration. I like exploring new places, and do whenever I get the chance, whether it be hills in Wales or a loch in the highlands of Scotland.
