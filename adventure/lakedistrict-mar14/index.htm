Lake District, 15th-16th March 2014

WE'RE GOING ON AN ADVENTURE

Route

  • Day one (approx. 14-16 miles):

    • Pick up Alex and Maddie from 161
    • Head to maccies on macon way and pick up breakfast
    • Pick up Gayhair from Sandbach
    • Drive for ages
    • Get to carpark in Far Sawry, park up and start walking
    • Heading north, alongside lake windermere, towards ambleside
    • Once we get to ambleside, start heading south-east, towards windermere
    • At windermere, head east towards staveley
    • Camp in staveley, hopefully go the beer festival at hawkshead brewery

  • Day two (approx. 8 miles):

    • Get up, nurse hangover with food and coffee, pack up
    • Head south-west towards bowness-on-windermere
    • Get the ferry from bowness to far sawry (50p, every 20mins)
    • Get back to carpark, pack and leave for home

Camping

We will be stopping at a campsite near staveley (little village, a few pubs and a brewery). We're booked into Pound Farm (~30min walk to beer festival). We need to get to the site by 5PM, and I've booked us in at £12pp.

Info/notes

  • Going to need to have:
    • Boots
    • Tent (these can be heavy, and carrying a popup is probably not practical)
    • Food/drink (ration packs are pretty good)
    • Cooking equipment (hexamine stoves are about a fiver with enough fuel for the weekend, you will need pans and stuff)
    • Change of clothes and warm/waterproof stuff (but not your whole fucking wardrobe!)
    • Sleeping bag
    • Decent bag to carry it all in
  • This could be a lot of weight for some people - think about what you pack (you probably don't need that kitchen sink)
  • It might be wet/miserable. I hope not, but do bear that in mind. Might be worth putting all your stuff in waterproof bags before it goes in your backpack.
  • Total cost is £22, plus change for parking and ferry.
    • £10 fuel
    • £12 camping fee
